About Us

Our Mission

The Palestine Solidarity Project is dedicated to advocating for justice, human rights, and freedom for the Palestinian people. Our mission is to challenge the oppression faced by Palestinians through education, grassroots activism, and international campaigns. We aim to raise awareness about the illegal occupation, forced displacement, and human rights violations committed against Palestinian communities.

  • Promote Awareness: Educate the public on the history and reality of the Palestinian struggle.
  • Challenge Misinformation: Counter media bias and ensure the truth about Palestine reaches the world.
  • Boycott, Divestment & Sanctions (BDS): Support the nonviolent economic and cultural boycott of companies complicit in oppression.
  • Defend Palestinian Rights: Advocate for freedom, justice, and equality, upholding international law and human rights.
  • End Apartheid: Campaign against the apartheid policies and systemic discrimination imposed on Palestinians.

Our movement stands in solidarity with Palestinians fighting for liberation, and we call on individuals, organizations, and governments to take action. We believe in the power of collective resistance and strive for a future where Palestine is free, and its people can live with dignity and justice.

Our Opposition

Challenging Injustice and Oppression

As a movement dedicated to justice and human rights, we strongly oppose all forms of oppression, including the illegal occupation of Palestine, systemic apartheid policies, and the violent suppression of Palestinian voices. Our opposition is rooted in the values of international law, human dignity, and freedom from oppression.

Taking Action

We call on governments, organizations, and individuals to take meaningful action against these injustices by supporting Palestinian-led resistance efforts, lobbying policymakers, and actively participating in the BDS movement.
